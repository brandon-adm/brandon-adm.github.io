Nintendo Switch OLED

The Nintendo Switch OLED is an upgraded version of the original Nintendo Switch, featuring a vibrant 7-inch OLED display that delivers deeper colors, richer contrast, and enhanced visual clarity. This model is perfect for handheld gaming, with its slimmer bezels and vivid screen that brings games to life. It also includes improved audio for both handheld and tabletop modes, making gaming even more immersive.

The system has 64GB of internal storage, double that of the original model, allowing for more games and media to be stored directly on the device. The dock has also been enhanced with a wired LAN port for more stable online play when in TV mode. The adjustable wide stand provides better support and flexibility, offering more comfortable viewing angles during tabletop play.

Despite the upgraded features, the Nintendo Switch OLED maintains compatibility with all previous Nintendo Switch games and accessories, keeping the same versatility that allows players to switch between handheld, tabletop, and docked modes effortlessly. It's a sleek and polished upgrade ideal for both new users and those seeking an enhanced portable gaming experience.
